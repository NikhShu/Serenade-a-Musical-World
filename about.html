Serenade Platform Rules

Serenade's mission is to unlock the potential of human creativity - by giving a million creative artists the opportunity to live off their art and billions of fans the opportunity to enjoy and be inspired by it. We believe achieving this mission on our platform is made possible by welcoming different forms of artistic expression, ideas, perspectives, and voices. This may mean that some content on our platform may not be to each individual's liking or is content that Serenade endorses.
However, that doesn't mean that anything goes on our platform. In addition to the terms that you agreed to governing your use of our services, these rules help ensure that everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience.


Reporting content on Serenade

At Serenade we work hard to elevate creative expression and want our community to safely present their authentic selves, but that does not mean that anything goes.
Our long-standing Platform Rules outline what is and isn't allowed on Serenade. We prioritize the review of content that impacts minors, presents a greater risk of offline harm, or may be illegal.
